## Target Markets

### 1. Supply Chain Management

**Problem**: Companies managing 100+ warehouses and thousands of shipments lack unified real-time visibility.

**GLX Monitor Solution**:
- Real-time shipment tracking across all locations
- Automated alerts for delays, capacity issues, compliance problems
- Predictive analytics for demand forecasting
- Blockchain audit trail for transparency and fraud prevention

**Target Customers**: 3PL providers, freight forwarders, manufacturing supply chains

### 2. Air Traffic Control Systems

**Problem**: ATC systems rely on fragmented tools that don't provide unified network views. Coordination delays cost millions.

**GLX Monitor Solution**:
- Distributed flight data management with blockchain security
- Real-time airspace visualization showing all aircraft
- Automated conflict detection before problems arise
- Integration with weather, maintenance, and ground systems

**Target Customers**: Airlines, airport operations, regional ATC systems

**Validated Lead**: Warm introduction to Delta Airlines operations team

### 3. Logistics Operations

**Problem**: Multi-modal logistics (trucks, trains, ships, planes) require switching between 5+ different monitoring tools.

**GLX Monitor Solution**:
- Unified view of all transportation modes
- Intelligent routing and resource optimization
- Real-time KPI tracking (delivery rates, costs, efficiency)
- Stakeholder coordination platform

**Target Customers**: Interstate movers, freight companies, last-mile delivery

**Validated Lead**: Positive feedback from interstate moving companies
